For my new portfolio, I wanted it to be a little more complex than my previous portfolio. I wanted a textured background to bring character to it, and I wanted to try my hand at title bars. I also wanted a "retro" sort of theme and it shows in the font and social icons. Overall, it gave me more practice on div layers and CSS.

We were required to use Twitter Bootstrap for this assignment, and I really enjoyed using it. This idea was not previously mocked up on Photoshop, the idea actually came to me very spontaneously. However, photoshop was used for the logo.
