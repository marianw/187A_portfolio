Forecast page


Details
My goal for this page was to make it as minimalistic as possible, but also still aesthetically pleasing. Weather should be simple to read, not hard- that was the goal I was aiming for. I made each weather forecast in a rectangle to give the semblence of a "card." I decided that the icon should do all the representing and made the temperature font larger than normal. When the sign-up button is clicked, a box pops up for you to enter information.

In the final version, the weather logo on the sign-up page had texture added to it. The sign-up button was also editted a little to make it pop-out more. Every page was mocked up in Photoshop prior to coding it.

It's viewable here at: http://weathertmr.com and the forecast page at: http://weathertmr.com/92092
