Details
This was my very first portfolio. I wanted to make something simple and for it to stand out at the same time. The home page is not consistent with the rest of the site as I wanted it to be a sort of "splash page" to my website. It has very little text and there's color variation for certain words to pop out. I was at a loss as to what to do with the "portfolio" page. I wanted to organize it without complicating the CSS too much, so in the end it became a list format more or less without any secondary page linking.

I made this page from scratch, sketching the layout first in a notebook. I didn't mock it up in Photoshop and just went based on my sketch. I already knew I wanted the background to be white and the text to be grey and black.

I wanted the portfolio to showcase different techniques I could do, which includes some minor Javascript to fade in and out the page.

The site is viewable here at: http://marianw.com
